Run 4
Query: constant tiredness causes

doc_115 (public Verywell excerpt, T3) became raw rank #1 with sim=0.843, the highest semantic score observed among the public-source fatigue entries.

After weighting, doc_115 dropped to weighted rank #5 because the T3 multiplier reduced its final score from 0.843 to 0.590.

doc_113 (public Mayo excerpt, T1) became weighted rank #1.
doc_110 (controlled T1 fatigue text) became weighted rank #2.
doc_114 (public WebMD excerpt, T2) remained weighted rank #3.

Observed pattern:
public-source T3 wording can dominate raw semantic similarity when query phrasing matches directly
tier weighting still suppresses lower-tier material even when raw relevance is strongest
all three public-source tiers are now visibly represented across Phase 4C runs
